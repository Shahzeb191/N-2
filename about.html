The Beginning

Born from a Passion
for Pure Flavour

N² was born in 2018 from a simple, radical idea: that vegetarian food deserves the same reverence, creativity, and craft as any fine dining experience in the world. Founded in Aonla, Bareilly, N² set out to prove that a meal without meat can be the most extraordinary meal of your life.

Our founders — brothers Nikhil and Neeraj Sharma — grew up in a family where food was language, love, and legacy. After years working in the finest kitchens of Mumbai and Delhi, they returned home with one mission: to bring world-class vegetarian fine dining to the heart of UP.

Every wall in N² tells that story. Every dish carries that intent. Every evening is a quiet act of devotion to the craft.

Our Philosophy

Three Pillars of N²

Every decision at N² — from the sourcing of an ingredient to the plating of a dish — flows from these three core beliefs.

01
Purity

We source only the finest seasonal produce — directly from farms we trust. No artificial flavours, no preservatives, no compromise on the integrity of what goes into your body. Purity is not a policy here. It is a promise.

02
Precision

Our chefs train obsessively in classical technique while pushing the boundaries of Indian cuisine. Every gram measured, every temperature calibrated, every texture deliberate. Precision is how we turn ingredients into emotion.

03
Passion

We believe food should feel like an event. From the moment you walk through our doors to the final bite of dessert — every moment is crafted to linger in your memory long after the evening ends.

Chef Arjun Sharma Executive Chef & Co-founder
Meet the Chef

The Hands Behind
Every Masterpiece

Chef Arjun Sharma is the creative force at the heart of N². With over 14 years of experience across the finest kitchens in India — including stints at The Leela Palace and Taj Mahal New Delhi — he returned home to Bareilly with a vision that transcended everything he had seen before.

"Vegetarian cooking is not a limitation," he says. "It is the purest canvas. Every texture, every depth of flavour — you have to earn it. There are no shortcuts." That philosophy is evident in every dish that leaves his kitchen.

Our Commitment

The Pure Veg
Promise

At N², "pure vegetarian" is not a marketing phrase. It is the deepest expression of who we are. From the first ingredient sourced to the last garnish placed, every element of our food is held to the strictest vegetarian standard.

Our kitchen is 100% vegetarian — no eggs, no cross-contamination, no compromise. Our supply chain is audited quarterly. And our team is trained to uphold this commitment as a matter of honour.

Certified 100% Pure Vegetarian
100% Pure Vegetarian · No Eggs · No Cross-Contamination
Farm-to-Table Sourcing

We work directly with verified organic farms in Uttarakhand and UP. No middlemen, no cold-stored produce, no exceptions.

Dedicated Veg Kitchen

Our entire kitchen infrastructure — from cookware to cutting boards — is dedicated exclusively to vegetarian preparation.

No Artificial Additives

We use no artificial colours, flavour enhancers, or preservatives. Every flavour you taste has been earned through technique, not chemistry.

Quarterly Audited

Our sourcing and preparation process is reviewed every quarter by an independent food safety auditor. Transparency is non-negotiable.

Our Journey

Six Years of Milestones

2018
The First Table

N² opens its doors in Aonla with 12 covers, a 22-item menu, and a dream bigger than the dining room itself.

2019
Chef's Table Launched

Introduction of the exclusive 7-course Chef's Table experience — now fully booked 3 weeks in advance.

2020
Survived & Thrived

During lockdown, N² pivoted to curated home-delivery fine dining kits — reaching 400+ households across Bareilly.

2022
Expansion & Recognition

Expanded to 40 covers. Featured in Condé Nast Traveller India's "Best Vegetarian Restaurants" list.

2024
The N² of Today

A destination restaurant. 80+ dishes. 15,000+ guests served. And still growing, still pushing, still obsessed.
